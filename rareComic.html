Rare Comics at ComiCore

Limited Edition or Really Old?

Rare comics are not for the feint of heart. Rarity is determined by multiple things, misprints, age, first edition's and much more! Thanks to our ever growing client base we have been able to aquire some of the more exquisite and rare comics. For example we have collected the original copy of the death of superman comic. A classically rare comic. Unlike the casual reader rare comics are mostly for collectors and as such are not cheap. Every week we offer an opportunity to you to collect some of our more rare comics. For more details you can check out our auction page, with dates for auctions posted up to a month in advanced. I hope to see all you comic collectors there.
