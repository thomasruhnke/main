ComiCore

Who are we?

ComiCore is a dedicated family owned business, focused solely on the collecting and selling aspects of comic books. Having been one of the primary comic book collecting business' in all of Quebec, we know our way around auctions, aquiring rare or antique comics or simply the casual reader. We know our way around comic books and we know how to get them. Want to sell a comic email us at comicore@gmail.com or meet us in person. We are an excited bunch of people, like you, who love comics. And we want to spread that love with others. Which is why we do what we do and have been doing it for some time. If it weren't for all the dedicated individuals out there who love comics as much as us. We never would have made it as far as we have made it.

Inside this website we have much to offer. In our auction page we talk to you about the basics of how they work. In our casual comics page we talk about comic reading and new readers as well. We also have our hourspage. And we also talk about our employees. And so much more you should really come check us out.
