ComiCore's Auction

This part of the website is where we notify and update when we will be holding auctions for some of the more rare comics. Now we will just touch up on how our auctions work. When you are at the auction there is a room which is usually rentable for public use. This room will not be rentable for the duration of the days that we have auctions. Next you may take a seat where you wish. There are dry erase markers and dry eraseable signs at the foot of each seat. This is where you will write your bid amount afterwards, the bid wars commence.

Our next auction will take place January 23rd 2024.
